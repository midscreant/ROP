SELAH CHILDREN'S MINISTRY

PROGRAM DESCRIPTION:

SELAH is a children’s program that exists with the goal of providing quality, Jesus- centered programing for inner city and refugee children on and around East Colfax. We have learned that the best way to interact with the at risk and refugee littles and their families is by meeting them literally where they are! We are currently meeting every Monday at the apartment complexes they live in, and engage littles by playing games, doing prize giveaways, assisting with math and reading skills, and most importantly, by sharing a story from God's living Word and praying with the littles and their families. God is allowing us to come alongside Him and share many stories from His word, bless families with physical and spiritual needs, and so much more. We use the following tactics to teach God’s Word to the kids, many of whom have little to no church involvement or knowledge of the Lord at all:

  • Games
  • Songs with actions
  • Activities / Worksheets
  • Crafts
  • Incentives and prizes
  • Visual, hands-on lessons
  • Snacks
