CRISIS
FOOD

If you were unable to participate in ROPâ€™s Tuesday Food Bank, ROP does make available crisis food for you and your family. There will be a basic sign-up form to fill out, and we will be asking about allergies or food prep limitations so we can offer you things you can utilize.

ROP attempts to provide food for your family for a couple of days. Unfortunately, this service can only be made available to an individual or household once a month.

Basic non-perishable items will be made available per what we have on hand from week to week. Frozen meats, dairy, or perishable items are rarely available after our regular Tuesday Food Bank Day.

If possible, it is best to call ahead to 720-859-2513 to set an appointment, as a crisis bag can only be assembled if office personnel are available.
