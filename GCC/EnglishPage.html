ENGLISH CLASSES

PROGRAM DESCRIPTION:

We offer English classes to the refugee and immigrant population in the surrounding neighborhoods, to help them overcome the language barriers they face living in the United States. Students are assessed and divided into four class levels, based on their English proficiency in speaking, listening, reading, and writing. We utilize the Cambridge Ventures curriculum in each level, and students are able to advance to the next level at the end of each school year.
