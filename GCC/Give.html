CONTRIBUTE

VISION

Financial gifts to ROP from churches, small groups and friends enhance the quality and standards ROP strives for in building God’s kingdom along East Colfax in Aurora, CO. Every gift matters, no matter the size, Through annual giving or one-time Gifts to ROP, the missionaries on staff, or the specific program you are passionate about, you enrich adults and students’ lives and the positive community we are trying to build. When you are making a gift, there are a variety of payment methods available to you. Additionally, there are opportunities to reach out with non-momentary items to a family or your choice of programs, which are tax deductible Gifts in Kind.
