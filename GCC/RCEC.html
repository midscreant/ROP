ABOUT RESTORATION COUNSELING ON EAST COLAX(RCEC)

Integrating mind, body and spirit to impact REAL CHANGE.

Led by Executive Director, Deb Ford, MA, LAC, LPC, CHTT, Restoration Counseling on East Colfax serves individuals trapped in addictions, abuse and human trafficking.

The RCEC goal is to provide each client with the education, skills and abilities to live a life full of hope, purpose and real change.

What do we mean by real change?

“Change always starts in your mind. The way you think determines the way you feel, and the way you feel influences the way you act.” - Rick Warren

RCEC believes that real change is a result of changing our thinking, changing our behavior, developing the skills to make healthy choices and living in our true identity instead of the one we previously created.
