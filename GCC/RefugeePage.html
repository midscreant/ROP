REFUGEE RESOURCES AND OUTREACH

PROGRAM DESCRIPTION:

Our refugee resource and outreach ministry works to accomplish ROP's mission of meeting needs, building relationships, sharing the love of God, and transforming lives through Jesus Christ within the growing refugee population of East Colfax. Our work is driven by connecting with our refugee neighbors and assisting them with all aspects of their resettlement in our community. This task includes evangelism; discipleship; seminary level Bible training and leadership development; tutoring; development of healthy, strong Bible-based churches; as well as mentoring and supporting our refugee neighbors in all aspects of their lives.
